History

The Northern Conference boosts of having 5 Schools. One high schools by the name of Sedaven High and five PrimarySchools namely, Sedaven Primary, Paterson Park Primary School and Wesrand Primary Scgool and Presda Primary School.

Mission

Our mission is to provide disadvantaged, learning disabled children and youth with access to education; vocational skills training, employment opportunities, and necessary support services. We aim to restore the image of Christ in our learners.

Organisation Overview

The Northern Confence employs about 50 teachers and other support staff members.

General Information

  • Remedial with specialized education offering Grades 1-9
  • Practical Skill Focused Subjects: Mathematics,Business Economics and Commercials for the High School and then all primary subjects
  • All our schools are accredited by the Department of Basic Education and Training Gauteng.
